LifeReminders Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

In short: LifeReminders stores all your data on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell your personal information. Voice processing happens entirely on-device.

Data Storage

All reminders, family member profiles, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device and is not uploaded to any server.

Voice Input

LifeReminders uses Apple's on-device speech recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer) to convert your voice into text. All voice processing happens locally on your iPhone. No audio recordings or transcripts are sent to our servers or any third party.

Notifications

LifeReminders uses Apple's local notification system to send you reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No push notification server is involved.

Advertising (Ad-Supported Plan)

If you choose the free ad-supported plan, LifeReminders displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use certain device information (such as advertising identifiers) to serve relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

The free plan (without ads) and the Pro plan do not display any advertisements and do not share any data with advertising networks.

Analytics & Tracking

LifeReminders does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or user tracking mechanisms beyond what is required for ad delivery in the ad-supported plan.

In-App Purchases

LifeReminders Pro is available as a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party. The only exception is the advertising data described above, which applies only to the ad-supported plan.

Children's Privacy

LifeReminders does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as a general-purpose reminder tool for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
