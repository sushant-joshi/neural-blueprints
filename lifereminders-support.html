Getting Started

Adding Reminders

Two ways to add reminders:

  • Voice: Tap the blue microphone button and speak naturally. Say something like "Mom's passport expires June 2028" or "Oil change due next month."
  • Manual: Tap the keyboard button, pick a category, fill in the details.

Three Views

  • Dashboard (swipe left/right to switch) — reminders by urgency
  • Timeline — reminders by time horizon
  • Calendar — week, month, or year view

Completing Reminders

Swipe right on any reminder to mark it as done. Recurring reminders (birthdays, bills, passports) automatically schedule the next occurrence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not getting notifications?

Go to Settings (in the app) and check that Push Notifications shows "Enabled." If not, tap "Enable" to open iOS Settings and allow notifications for LifeReminders.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings (in the app) → Your Plan → "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Is my data backed up?

LifeReminders stores all data on your device. Your data is included in iCloud device backups and iTunes/Finder backups. There is no separate cloud sync.

How do I remove ads?

Upgrade to LifeReminders Pro ($4.99 one-time) in Settings → Your Plan → "Upgrade to Pro." This removes all ads and gives you unlimited reminders.

Does voice input require an internet connection?

No. All voice processing happens on-device using Apple's speech recognition. No audio is sent to any server.
